How it works

Brace yourself for the complexity.

1

Press the hotkey

Hit ⌥ Space from anywhere. A little waveform shows up. Dict is listening.

2

Talk

Say whatever. Be incoherent. Use filler words. Speak in Portuguese mid-sentence if you want. We won't judge.

3

Text appears

Clean text lands where your cursor was. That's literally it. No step four.
