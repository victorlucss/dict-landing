Features

Built for the way you work

App-Aware AI

Adapts tone automatically. Casual in Slack, formal in Mail, code-aware in VS Code.

99 Languages

Powered by Whisper. Auto-detect or pick your language. Switch seamlessly mid-conversation.

Code Mode

Dictate code naturally. Auto-formats to camelCase or snake_case with proper indentation.

Privacy Mode

Run fully offline. Local Whisper + local LLM. Your voice never leaves your machine.

Instant Paste

Text appears wherever your cursor is. No copy-paste needed. Under 500ms latency.

Custom Dictionary

Teach Dict your names, jargon, and acronyms. Never misspell your company name again.

How it works

Three steps. Zero friction.

1

Press the hotkey

Hit ⌥ Space from any app. Dict starts listening instantly.

2

Speak naturally

Talk like you normally would. AI cleans up grammar and removes filler words.

3

Text appears

Polished text is pasted right where your cursor is. No extra steps.

Privacy first

Your voice stays yours

Dict can run entirely offline. Use local Whisper for transcription and a local LLM for processing. No data leaves your machine, ever.

  • On-device Apple Speech recognition
  • Local Whisper via whisper.cpp
  • Compatible with Ollama & LM Studio
  • Zero external dependencies in core
