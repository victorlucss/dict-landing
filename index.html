What it does

No groundbreaking innovation here. Just a thing that works.

LLM cleanup

An LLM reads your mess of filler words and "ums" and turns it into something you wouldn't be embarrassed to send.

Português & English

Two languages. That's what we speak, that's what it supports. If you need Mandarin, this isn't the app for you.

Code mode

For when you want to dictate code like a psychopath. It'll try its best with camelCase and snake_case.

Runs offline

Local Whisper + local LLM. Your voice never leaves your Mac. Not because we're noble, just because it's simpler.

Instant paste

Text shows up where your cursor is. No clipboard dance, no switching windows. Just appears.

Custom dictionary

Teach it your weird proper nouns and company jargon so it stops autocorrecting your CEO's name.

How it works

Brace yourself for the complexity.

1

Press the hotkey

Hit ⌥ Space from anywhere. A little waveform shows up. Dict is listening.

2

Talk

Say whatever. Be incoherent. Use filler words. Speak in Portuguese mid-sentence if you want. We won't judge.

3

Text appears

Clean text lands where your cursor was. That's literally it. No step four.

Privacy

We don't want your data

Seriously. There's no server. No analytics. No account. No telemetry. Dict can run fully offline because we couldn't be bothered to build a backend.

  • On-device Apple Speech recognition
  • Local Whisper via whisper.cpp
  • Works with Ollama & LM Studio
  • No account, no sign-up, no nothing
